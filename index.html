About Us

The WN Hub is an online communication platform for game industry designed to help companies reach their business goals online: learn about other players on the market, host video meetings, strengthen existing and establish new partnerships, exchange news and knowledge, build a strong HR brand, and a lot more.

The best part about it - no travelling is required. The only asset you need is your laptop or smartphone.

Solutions

Why host your next video game event on WN Hub?

WN Hub is an online platform for video game events, both B2B and B2C, where you can create an engaging virtual experience for your audience.

A team with over eight years of experience in gaming events will help you setup a landing page, sell tickets, gather attendees and analyze results - we'll do everything to keep you and your participants happy!

Request your offer at welcome@wn.media.
